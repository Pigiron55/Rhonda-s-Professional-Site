Splashpage Mockup Oddjob Mobile App

Oddjob Signup page

I created a responsive splashpage for the Oddjob app. The splashpage has a catchy marketing slogan and a simple signup form.

>>Splashpage Mobile<<

Using basic principles of UI/UX design and responsive web design I was able to create a page that looks good on desktop and mobile devices.

The yellow and black color scheme creates an eye catching and memorable design. The Bowler hat was chosen as a callback to the classic James Bond henchman, Oddjob.
