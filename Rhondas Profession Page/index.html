Hi, I am Rhonda Williams

Therapist, LPCC, NCC

What I do

In-office Therapy

I help clients work through the difficulties in life in a one on one office setting. I provide services to treat and manage PTSD, anxiety, depression, stress, sexual and relational concerns, parenting, dicorce, relationships, OCD, grief and trauma.

Tele-Health Therapy

I can work with clients who are no longer local but who want to continue their therapy with me remotely through tele-health consultations.

Group Based Therapy

I run regular programs for groups of people whether it is family counseling or school based programs for at-risk kids

Who I am

Licensed Professional Clinical Counselor based out of Glasgow, KY

I am a level 2 Gottman trained, EMDR certified Therapuetic Rehabilitation program provider specializing in treating children with behavioral issues or trauma.

I am a board member for CASA of SKY, A court appointed special advocate. I also volunteer for Hosparus Health.

I am also a mother of two wonderful kids and one adorable dog. Being a therapist is my calling and I love coming to work every single day.
