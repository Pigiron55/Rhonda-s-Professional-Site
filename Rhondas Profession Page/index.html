In-office Therapy

I help clients work through the difficulties in life in a one on one office setting. I provide services to treat and manage PTSD, anxiety, depression, stress, sexual and relational concerns, parenting, dicorce, relationships, OCD, grief and trauma.

Tele-Health Therapy

I can work with clients who are no longer local but who want to continue their therapy with me remotely through tele-health consultations.
